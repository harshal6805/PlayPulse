Help & Getting Started

Everything you need to know to get the most out of PlayPulse. Up and running in under a minute.

Quick Start

Three steps to go from zero to tracking your first playlist:

1

Sign in with Google

Click "Get Started" on the homepage and sign in with your Google account. It takes one click — no passwords to remember, and your data syncs across all your devices instantly.

2

Add a YouTube Playlist

Go to the Playlists tab and click "Add Playlist". Paste any YouTube playlist URL. PlayPulse will automatically fetch all videos, thumbnails, and durations for you.

Tip: You can also add playlists from the Dashboard
3

Watch, Track & Earn XP

Click any video to open the built-in player. Videos auto-mark as complete when you finish them. Take notes, earn XP, build streaks, and watch your progress bar hit 100%!

Dashboard

Your Learning Hub

The Dashboard is your home screen. At a glance you can see:

  • Daily Stats — Minutes studied, videos completed, current streak, and total XP
  • Daily Goal — Set a study goal (in minutes) and track your progress through the day
  • Smart Resume — Jump right back to the last video you were watching
  • Recent Playlists — Quick access to your most active playlists
  • Weekly Chart — Visual overview of your study patterns this week

Playlists & Videos

Managing Playlists

Each playlist can be tagged and prioritized (High / Medium / Low). Use filters to find playlists quickly. Click the edit icon to:

  • Sync with YouTube — Fetch new videos added to the playlist
  • Mark All Done / Reset — Bulk-complete or reset all videos
  • Export Data — Download your progress as a JSON file

Watching Videos

Click any video to open the built-in player. You can:

  • Adjust playback speed (0.75x to 5x)
  • Use Picture-in-Picture mode
  • Navigate between videos with Prev / Next buttons
  • Take rich-text notes that auto-save to the cloud
  • Export notes as Markdown

Analytics

Track Your Progress

The Analytics tab gives you a deep look at your learning habits:

  • 365-Day Heatmap — GitHub-style activity heatmap showing daily study activity
  • Weekly Study Minutes — Bar chart of your study time over the past week
  • Monthly Completions — How many videos you've completed each month
  • Total Hours Over Time — Cumulative watch time chart
  • XP Growth — Track your XP progression over time

XP, Streaks & Achievements

Gamification System

PlayPulse rewards consistent learning:

  • XP — Earn experience points for completing videos. Level up as you accumulate XP.
  • Streaks — Complete at least one video per day to maintain your streak. Miss a day? Use a streak recovery token.
  • Combos — Study on consecutive days to build your combo multiplier: 3+ days = 2x XP, 7+ days = 3x XP.
  • Badges — Unlock achievements like "First Blood" (first video), "Marathon" (10 videos in a day), and more.
  • Skill Tree — Visual progression system tied to your overall progress.

Pomodoro Timer

Stay Focused

The built-in Pomodoro timer helps you study in focused intervals:

  • Default: 25 minutes focus, 5 minutes break (customizable)
  • Access from the topbar clock icon or the floating action button
  • Optional: auto-start when you play a video
  • Sound notifications when sessions end (can be muted)
