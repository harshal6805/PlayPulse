Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to PlayPulse. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

When you use PlayPulse, we may collect the following information:

  • Google Account Data: Your name, email address, and profile picture when you sign in with Google.
  • Usage Data: Playlist progress, watch history, study statistics, XP, streaks, and notes you create within the app.
  • Device Information: Browser type, screen resolution, and operating system for improving user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the PlayPulse service.
  • Sync your progress across devices.
  • Calculate and display your study statistics, achievements, and streaks.
  • Improve the app based on usage patterns.

4. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored in Google Firebase (Firestore). We use Firebase Authentication for sign-in, meaning we never store your Google password. All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS.

5. Third-Party Services

PlayPulse integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and data storage.
  • YouTube API: Fetching playlist and video metadata.
  • Formspree: Processing feedback and bug report form submissions.

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is only used to provide the PlayPulse service.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us through the feedback form.

8. Cookies

PlayPulse uses local storage (not cookies) to remember your theme preference and session state. No tracking cookies are used.

9. Children's Privacy

PlayPulse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the Feedback & Bug Report form in the app.
