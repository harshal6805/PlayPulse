About PlayPulse

We believe the best courses on the internet are already free — they're just scattered across YouTube playlists. PlayPulse gives you the tools to finish what you start.

The Problem We Solve

Everyone's been there — you find an amazing YouTube playlist with 40+ videos on a topic you're passionate about. You watch the first three, get distracted, and never come back. A month later, you can't even remember where you left off.

PlayPulse was built to fix exactly that. It transforms any YouTube playlist into a structured, trackable learning experience — complete with progress tracking, notes, analytics, and the gamification to keep you motivated.

Our Mission

To make self-directed learning on YouTube feel as structured and rewarding as any paid course — for free, forever. No paywalls, no premium tiers. Just a focused app that helps you learn better.

What We Stand For

🎯

Focus First

Every feature exists to help you stay on track. No distractions, no bloat — just the tools you need to finish playlists.

🆓

Free Forever

Education should be accessible. PlayPulse is completely free with no hidden costs, ads, or premium gates.

🔒

Privacy Matters

Your data stays yours. We don't sell or share your information. We use Firebase's enterprise-grade security.

🎮

Learning = Fun

XP, streaks, badges, and combos make learning rewarding. Small wins compound into huge progress.
